## How to work with me

👋 I'm a mix of **German and Italian** — structure and precision on one side, creativity and improvisation on the other. Sometimes that feels like mixing oil and water.

Expect directness, a structured mindset, curiosity, eagerness for innovation, and occasionally dry (usually bad) humor and memes.

I value **integrity, reliability, consistency, curiosity, and innovation**.

Integrity to me means **personal trust and openness**. The best idea should win, regardless of who proposed it.

💬 I appreciate **direct communication**. If something looks wrong or could be improved, please say it openly and ideally talk to me directly rather than using indirect loops or waiting for formal feedback cycles.  
If my feedback ever feels too direct or my communication style doesn’t work well for you, please tell me.

📝 I prefer **async and written communication** when possible. It helps keep things clear and documented.

⚡ I often like to **move quickly and iterate**, depending on the risk and impact of a decision.

🤝 I value people who **keep commitments and work consistently**. Reliable collaboration makes teams and systems work much better.

I sometimes move **very fast** and can be a bit impatient — feel free to slow me down if needed.

🔍 I also tend to notice **small details and broken things quickly**. This can be useful, but sometimes means I focus on issues others haven't spotted yet.

💡 If you disagree with me technically, let's **talk it through and find the best option**.

I'm happy to help with **architecture, operational issues, recurring problems, cross-team topics, or tricky situations — even outside engineering**. When helping others, I usually prefer **guiding rather than giving immediate answers**.
